About me

My interest in front-end web development started in high school while procrastinating to learn C++. After watching multiple tutorials about C++, I stumbled upon a tutorial about HTML and CSS. It was the first time I saw that programming can create colours and shapes, everything was visual. As a result, I spent that entire night watching videos of people creating cool websites. After finishing high school and breaking up with C++, I moved to England to study Interactive Media at the University of York, where I have also learnt how to built websites. Now my goal is to create websites for real bussinesses, not only the ones I create in my head. :)
